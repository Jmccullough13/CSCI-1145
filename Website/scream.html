Thoughts

This is one of the movies on this list that I probably should have seen years ago but never did. I mentioned before that I’m not a fan of slasher movies for all of the tropes started in Black Christmas that carried on and infested so many other movies. This movie is a parody that turned those tropes on their head, making fun of them while committing the same sin, and I loved it. They had all of the stupidity, and cringe of a normal slasher flick, but instead of leaning on it, they swerved at full speed into it. The best exemplification of this was when Randy was watching the movie and yelling at the screen to watch out, look behind them, and Ghostface walks in right behind him and raises the knife while Randy continues to yell at the screen. All of the gratuitous gore and violence went beyond the other slasher movies, but it served as a parody of those movies in its exaggeration. This movie was brilliant, and I wish I hadn’t known every single plot point going into it. Knowing didn’t lessen the movie, but it would have been a better experience if I hadn’t known beforehand. C’est la vie, perhaps I’ll learn from this.
