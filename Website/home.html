About Me:

I am an avid horror buff and have always been so, as long as I can remember, anyway. I remember, as a child, checking out books on every Universal Studio monster movie and reading them relentlessly. From there, I expanded my interests to Godzilla properties, and then onto vampires, zombies, aliens, and any other horror genre that I could get my hands on, as long as it contained fantastical elements. I first watched Gremlins when I was eight, and my life was forever changed. Side note: While I say that I’m a horror buff, I generally try and stay away from formulaic slasher movies, especially those with five thousand sequels. I also don’t count psychological thrillers as horror movies, because while they contain horror elements, horror isn’t what they’re focused on.
