Gravity Falls

2 Seasons

Gravity Falls is one of, if not the, greatest animated shows of all time, and it has a lot to compete against. What starts as a light-hearted children's mystery romp unwinds into an epic and gripping coming-of-age saga that has something for everyone! The show has humor, mystery, twists, drama, and even horror. As the show itself states when it cheekily breaks the fourth wall in one of its dozens of meta moments: "...has a big mystery element and a lot of humor that goes over kids' heads!"
